About Brand

At THATHA’S, we serve a diverse range of non-vegetarian dishes, specializing in flavorful chicken and mutton preparations. Our focus is on delivering authentic tastes using fresh ingredients and unique spices. Whether it's a family meal or a quick bite, THATHA’S promises a warm atmosphere and unforgettable flavors. En Virunthe Vaagai Soodum—our feast is a celebration of taste and tradition.

What i'm doing

  • Packaging

    Eco-friendly and sustainable packaging materials are preferred, featuring simple yet elegant branding. Packaging should be both functional and appealing, with a focus on practicality and style that reflects Thatha's commitment to quality and flavor.

  • Tone and Voice Consistent Messaging

    Content should be inviting, warm, and family-oriented. The voice is friendly yet confident, emphasizing tradition, hospitality, and quality. Focus on conveying a welcoming and enjoyable dining experience that highlights authentic non-vegetarian flavors.

  • Merchandise Design

    Incorporate traditional and cultural elements (like motifs, patterns, and regional symbols) without overshadowing the Thatha's brand. The design should evoke a sense of authenticity, quality, and taste, while maintaining a clean and modern look.

  • Brand Identity Logo Usage

    The Thatha's logo should be prominently displayed on all menus, packaging, promotional materials, and signage. Ensure the logo is always clear and visible, maintaining consistent sizing and color across all platforms.
